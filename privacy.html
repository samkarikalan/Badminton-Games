Privacy Policy for Club Scheduler

Effective Date: 2026-01-07

1. Information Collection

Club Scheduler does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. All information entered into the app remains locally stored on the user’s device.

2. Use of Information

Data entered by users (such as player names, schedules, and settings) is used only to operate app features and is never transmitted outside the device.

3. Data Sharing

No data is shared with third parties. The app does not use analytics, advertising SDKs, or cloud services.

4. Data Retention & Deletion

All app data remains on the device until cleared by the user or when the app is uninstalled. Uninstalling the app permanently removes all stored data.

5. Security

Because all data is stored locally, it is protected by the device’s operating system security features.

6. Children’s Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

Any changes to this Privacy Policy will be posted on this page.
